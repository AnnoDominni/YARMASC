About YARMASC

A Founder-Led Platform at the Intersection of Assets, Mobility & Intelligence

YARMASC is a business services and premium advisory platform established in Abu Dhabi, UAE — operating from Masdar City, one of the region's leading ecosystems for clean energy, sustainable urban development and technology-led enterprise.

We work with asset owners, investors, infrastructure stakeholders, aviation and mobility businesses, and enterprise clients across the UAE and GCC to bring clarity to complex decisions. Our focus spans smart built environments, advanced mobility, asset intelligence, operational studies, B2B sourcing coordination and strategic business advisory.

YARMASC combines practical operational insight with commercial discipline, lifecycle thinking and technology readiness. Through a curated expert-network model, we bring together specialist capability, market intelligence and trusted partner access — while maintaining direct oversight of strategy, quality, governance and delivery outcomes.

How We Work

The YARMASC Engagement Framework

A disciplined five-phase approach that turns ambiguity into structured action — and action into measurable outcomes.

PHASE 01
Discover
Understand the client's asset environment, strategic priorities, constraints and desired outcomes through structured discovery dialogue.
PHASE 02
Diagnose
Analyse asset intelligence, operational data, market signals and risk exposure to produce a clear diagnostic of the challenge and opportunity landscape.
PHASE 03
Structure
Design the advisory, sourcing or strategic framework — defining scope, approach, partner requirements and commercial governance before any commitment is made.
PHASE 04
Enable
Execute the agreed pathway — market entry, sourcing coordination, partner mapping, studies delivery or strategic documentation — with direct oversight.
PHASE 05
Optimise
Review outcomes, capture learnings and refine the operating model — building a cycle of continuous improvement across assets, operations and relationships.
