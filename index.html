YARMASC is a business services and premium advisory platform established in Abu Dhabi, the capital of the UAE and a global meeting point for investment, innovation, sustainability, and future mobility.

Operating from Masdar City, one of the region’s leading ecosystems for clean energy, sustainable urban development and technology-led enterprise, YARMASC is positioned to support organizations navigating the next generation of asset, mobility and operational challenges.

We work with asset owners, investors, infrastructure stakeholders, aviation and mobility businesses, and enterprise clients across the UAE and GCC to bring clarity to complex decisions.
